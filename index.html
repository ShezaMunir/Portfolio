aarzu: physical therapy for the disabled

A digital assistant for patients traveling to Lahore from far-off areas to undergo physical therapy at Pakistan Society for Rehabilitation of the Disabled (PSRD) hospital. The app allows doctors to make a pool of exercises for each patient and to track their daily progress. The patients could see the exercises (with tutorials) scheduled for the day and could mark them once done.

migraine management system

Uses biomarkers, digital diaries, and weather data to detect the onset of migraines. The wearable data includes heart rate, electrodermal activity, and temperature; while sleep and stress data are gathered from the digital diaries (5 patients). We conducted data analysis and found a strong correlation between EDA peaks of patients overnight and migraine onset in the morning.
