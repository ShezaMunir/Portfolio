Data Science and Machine Learning

deepfake detection dataset

The culmination of this project was marked by a paper submission to the Association for Computational Linguistics (ACL) Rolling Review. I spearheaded the creation of an extensive Urdu speech training dataset, consisting of approximately 37,000 audios, aimed at detecting deepfakes and addressing the growing threat of misinformation through audio manipulations on social media platforms. Leveraging Python, deep learning techniques, and advanced Text-to-Speech models (Vits TTS and Tacatron), the project aimed to enhance deepfake detection capabilities. Additionally, I led a team of over 40 students, strategically applying statistical methods to a phonetically rich corpus. This collaborative effort resulted in a dataset where an impressive one in three audios proved indistinguishable from authentic speech. These findings underscored the pivotal role of such datasets in advancing the effectiveness of deepfake detection algorithms.

migraine prediction system

In this project, I conducted a comprehensive analysis of a complex dataset involving 15 features, including longitudinal heart rate, EDA, stress, and environmental factors. The data, collected from wearables and digital diaries over the span of a year for five patients, aimed to identify recurring patterns. Subsequently, I employed machine learning (ML) and statistical methods in Python to design Long Short Term Memory (LSTM) models for time-series data. The objective was to predict migraines one day in advance, achieving an impressive accuracy rate of 88%.

aarzu: physical therapy for the disabled

In a project that garnered significant acclaim, I received recognition for the Best Project in CS 466-Fall 2021, an achievement that was further highlighted when the project was featured and presented in the LUMS Learning Institution Collaboration Showcase 2022. This endeavor involved a meticulous analysis, leveraging insights from interviews and contextual inquiries to identify crucial pain points in physiotherapy processes at the Pakistan Society for Rehabilitation of the Disabled hospital. Taking a proactive approach, I then conceptualized and implemented an mobile application specifically tailored for low-income, low-literate end users. The success of the application was evident in its remarkable task-completion rates, reaching 100% with doctors and 84.4% with patients.

Market Analysis: Effects of Current Events on Economy

In a comprehensive analysis of daily commodities datasets, I conducted data analysis using Python libraries, including pandas, numpy, and matplotlib. This exploration aimed to uncover robust correlations among economic indicators, providing valuable insights for decision-making in economic forecasting. Additionally, I delved into Natural Language Processing (NLP) on a news articles dataset. This involved incorporating Spearman rank order correlations and regression analysis to grasp the connections between real-world events and their influence on the global economic landscape, contributing to a comprehensive understanding of market trends.
